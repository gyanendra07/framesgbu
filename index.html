ABOUT US

Every college campus strives hard to create an atmosphere which promotes academic as well as co-curricular excellence.

Frames, the Photography Club of GBU, makes this come true. Our club focuses on enriching students' interest in photography and teaches how to capture moments into frames.

Our club conducts different activities for it's members round the year at the college. There are various events such as Photowalks around different places of the city, Photography Exhibitions on various genres and Training sessions for the club members. Our club also covers various major events of the college.
